Chapter 6
Beauty and the Beast 🌹

Oka raat...

Nuvvu naaku poori raat oka story cheppavu.

Aa story peru — "Beauty and the Beast."

Aa raat... aa story... naa gunde lo uniki poyindi 🌹

Aa raat nee cheppina prathi maata naa gunde lo uniki poyindi.

Naku anipinchindi — manam iddaram kadha kuda aa story laage undi ani.

Nuvvu Hindu... nenu Muslim.

Nuvvu naakante oka saal pedda.

Kaani prem mundu ivi anni chinna vishayalu laage anipinchaayi.

Nee prathi maata naaku sukoon ichhedi. Nee past ni nenu poortiga accept chesanu.

Endukante naaku nee happiness matra kaavali. 🌹

Aa Beauty and the Beast story nuvvu cheppinappudu... nenu realize chesanu.

Nuvvu Belle laanti ammayi — kind, beautiful, strong.

Nenu aa beast — rough outside, kaani nee love tho naa gunde lo oka rose bloom ayyindi. 🌹

Aa story maa story laage feel ayyindi naaku. Adi eppatiki marchipoledu.
