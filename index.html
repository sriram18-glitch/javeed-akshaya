Andamaina Rojulu ❤️

Aa rojulu naa life lo most beautiful memories.

Nenu wait chestha...

Nuvvu vastavu...

Oka choopu chalu...

Aa rojanta happy ga undevadini. 🌹

Mana madhya unna aa chinna moments ippatiki naa gundelo alane unnayi.

Prathi pilaka... prathi navvu... prathi maatukunna maata...

Anni naa life lo most precious things aipoyayi.

Nuvvu naa life lo vastagaane... prathi roju oka rose bloom avutundi naa gundelo.

Nee gurinchi alochistunnappudu naa face meedha smile vasthundi — teliyanattu undi kaani vasthundi.

Aa feeling nuvvu ichindi. Daani kosam forever thankful ga untanu. 🌹

Page 8
Aa Oka Maata

Oka roju...

Nuvvu nee life lo inkokarini preminchutunnanu ani cheppavu.

🥀

Aa maata naa gunde ni mukkalu chesindi.

Nenu pranam kanna ekkuvaga preminchina ammayi dooram aipoyindani ardham ayyindi.

Kani appudu kuda nenu nee happiness kosame korukunnanu.

Endukante nijamaina prema ante possessiveness kaadu.

Preminchina vaallu happy ga undadam choodadam.

"Nee navvu chusina prati sari naa gunde nokukundi."

"Kani nee pain chusina sari naa gunde inkaa nokukundi."

"Aa feeling ni explain cheyyadam naaku telidhu." 🌹
